# Ad Rating Practice - Sample Task Set

## Example 1  
**Query**: Buy affordable shoes online  
**Ad**: Sporty Sneakers – 50% Off  
**Rating**: HIGH  
**Why?** The ad is relevant and matches commercial search intent.
