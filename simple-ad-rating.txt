# Ad Rating Practice - Sample Task Set

## Example 1  
Query: Buy sports shoes online  
Ad: 50% off on running shoes – Shop Now  
Rating: HIGH  
Why? Ad is highly relevant to user's commercial intent.

---

## Example 2  
Query: Top Hindi movie songs  
Ad: Buy Smart TV at 30% off  
Rating: LOW  
Why? Ad is irrelevant to the entertainment/search intent.

---

## Example 3  
Query: Best Cooking Classes in Delhi  
Ad: Join Cooking Masterclass – Lajpat Nagar, ₹499/month  
Rating: HIGH  
Why? Ad matches local and informational-commercial intent.
